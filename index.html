Snally

Where cryptids code, data shapeshifts, and experiments escape the lab.

Have you ever seen a Snallygaster
A Maryland folklore creature assembled from rumor, media, and fear.
We borrow the name for projects that are stitched together and refuse to settle into a single shape. ?

Descriptions never agree, and that is the point: a stitched together thing of wings, beak, claws, and rumor. Born from contradiction. Always in flux.

Snally exists for projects like that.

Some sites wear neckties. This one shows up in neon sneakers, half finished prototypes tucked under its arm. Snally is a sandbox for strange web builds and offbeat, data driven sketches, where folklore trades campfire stories for JavaScript, CSS, Python, and Quarto.

Here, numbers turn playful. Interfaces bend. Ideas are explored out loud and allowed to remain unfinished.


Think of it as a garage after midnight. Lights on. Coffee brewing. Code flickering.

Wander in and see what hatched today.
