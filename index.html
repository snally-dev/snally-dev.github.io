About Snally

Have you ever seen a Snallygaster
A Mid-Atlantic cryptid shaped as much by early twentieth century newspapers as by folklore.
Some tellings carried the prejudices of their time.
We borrow the name for work that is stitched together, restless, and hard to pin down. ?

Descriptions never agree. Wings, beak, claws, rumor. A stitched-together thing built from contradiction and still mutating.

Snally is a sandbox for strange web builds, offbeat data work, and creative technical experiments, where folklore trades campfire stories for JavaScript, CSS, Python, and Quarto.

Interfaces bend. Numbers turn playful. Ideas get tested in public and can stay a little unfinished.

Think of it as a garage after midnight. Lights on. Coffee brewing. Code flickering. Wander in and see what hatched today.
